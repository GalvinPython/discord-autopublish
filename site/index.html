Auto Publish for Discord

Publish with no clicks

What is it?

Auto Publish is a Discord bot that when you invite it to your server, will automatically publish your announcements with no delay. No click involved, it just joins and does its tasks with no fuss

How does it work?

It's fairly simple actually! Just invite the bot and it'll listen for your messages and if it's a message from an announcement channel, it'll publish for you automatically!

Terms of Service

In accordance with Discord's rate limit for 10 published messages per hour per server, we allow the bot to be used in any server that doesn't exceed these limits. We reserve the right to block the bot from your server if it constantly exceeds this limit. Furthermore, the server must comply with Discord's Terms of Service and we reserve the right to block the bot from your server if we deem that it's unacceptable

Privacy Policy

We log every action from the bot so that we are able to identify errors and investigate why they happened. We do not log any other information, such as the message. Just if a message was published, and if an error happened, why
