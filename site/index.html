Auto Publish for Discord

Publish with no clicks

What is it?

Auto Publish is a Discord bot that when you invite it to your server, will automatically publish your announcements with no delay. No click involved, it just joins and does its tasks with no fuss

How does it work?

It's fairly simple actually! Just invite the bot and it'll listen for your messages and if it's a message from an announcement channel, it'll publish for you automatically!

Terms of Service

  • Discord has a rate limit of 10 published messages per hour per server.
  • You may use this in any server that complies with the Discord ToS.
  • You may be blocked from using the bot if your server exceeds the rate limit.

Privacy Policy

  • The bot requires the intent to look at messages in your server, but only to check if they are in announcement channels.
  • Every published message is logged (not the content, just the channel ID) to assist with issues.
  • Any messages attempted to be published are logged (just the channel and message IDs) to help with errors.
  • Logs are only there for the purposes of aiding you with any problems.
  • Any information not listed here isn't logged.
